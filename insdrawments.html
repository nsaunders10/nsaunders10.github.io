Insdrawments

Insdrawments is a fun little app that I made for Andriod and is available on the Google Play store here!
With Insdrawments you can draw three different instruments and then play them. The larger you draw the instrument the deeper the sound.

Draw a circle to make a drum.

Draw a square to make a keyboard.

Draw a triangle to make a cymbal.
