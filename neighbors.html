Project Vibe: Neighbors VR

Neighbors VR is sandbox tech demo designed for two players on one Vive system where players explore the surroundings to find and combine objects to destroy their neighbors house. This project was made as my final senior project at the Universty of Florida Digital Worlds Institute.
The videos below show the process of development for this project, starting with the inital pitch, then an update mid-way through, and last the final trailer for the game.
